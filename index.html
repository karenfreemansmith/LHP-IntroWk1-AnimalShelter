Currently Available:

ELMER

He's some pig! Unfortunatly, his previous owners had to move to a small apartment in the city and didn't have room for Elmer anymore. He's looking for a large backyard with plenty of mud holes to play in.

PETER

Little Peter Cottontail was caught in Farmer Jone's garden one too many times. Peter would like a home with a family who would feed him lots of carrots and can keep him safely away from the neighbor's yard.

HAMTARO

Hamtaro was severely traumatized by the cats in his last home and tends to be shy. He likes gentle children and would prefer a home without other pets.

GEORGE

George is a rescue animal. He was poached from the Everglades as an egg and is not capable of living in the wild. He needs a very special owner. Classes on alligator handling are available at a discount if you adopt George.

FOO-FOO

Foo-Foo was abandoned by the good fairy on our doorstep. He has a habbit of escaping most cages and loves hiding out in the forest terrorizing the field mice. He needs an owner who knows how to set boundaries.

LUKE & LEIA

These twins are looking for a warm and loving environment. They are sure to attract lots of attention from your friends and neighbors. Low upkeep.
